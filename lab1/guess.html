Number Guessing Game

I have picked a random number between 1 and 50. Enter A guess below and hit submit.

If your guess is too high it will appear in your previous guesses in red.
If your guess is too low it will appear in your previous guesses in blue.
If you guess correct it will appear in your previous guesses in green and you will recieve an alert notifying you you won.

You have 10 chances to guess the number or else you lose.
